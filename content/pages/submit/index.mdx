Hi there!

To submit a fundraiser to be published on the site, please send an email to mail@boborchard.com with the following information:

* Name of Fundraiser
* Short Description of the fundraiser
* Link to where people can take action
* Your name, phone number and email address

I will review them as they come in and let you know when your link has been added to the site.
